MALANG

Motto: Malang kuçeçwara

Malang city is a city located in the province of East Java, Indonesia. The second largest city in East Java after Surabaya and the 12th largest city in Indonesia. Malang City was founded on April 1, 1914, and Malang City is located on a plateau covering an area of 145.28 km.

What are the universities in Malang?

Malang city is the center of Peneikan city, this city has various universities such as Brawijaya University, Malang State University, Maulana Malik Ibrahim Islamic University and Malang State Polytechnic

What tribes are in Malang?

Malang city consists of various kinds of people from various ethnic groups and cultures. The population of Malang City reaches 895,387 people with the majority being Javanese and followed by the Madurese.

What are the cultures in Malang?

Malang has a lot of culture, from various historical relics, such as the ancient Kayutangan Church building, and has a "MALANG TEMPO DOELOE" festival and has a Malang Monument.
